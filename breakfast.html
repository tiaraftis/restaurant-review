BREAKFAST

Gables Bagels

$$

★★★★

Gables is a classic New Jersey bagel place and everything is fantastic. I love to get a ham and swiss bagel with their famous chipotle aioli. Their bagels are fresh and so soft and they also offer tons of bagel types and schmears so you can mix it up every visit.

Bloomington Bagels

$

★★★☆

Bloomington’s bagel scene is no joke, and Bloomington Bagels definitely earns its place on the list. The shop has a fun, lively vibe and an impressive lineup of bagels, schmears, and toppings. This spot is still a great go to for a quick, tasty, and customizable breakfast.

Lincoln Square Pancake House

$$

★★★★★

This is hands-down the best breakfast in town. I come here weekly with friends, and the food never misses. I always grab the cinnamon roll pancakes and we love sharing the chicken and waffle sliders. It’s warm, welcoming, and consistently delicious. A Bloomington must try!

Village Deli

$$

★★★★

The best breakfast spot on Kirkwood! Their Blueberry Lemon Poppyseed Pancakes are so light, fluffy, and the perfect balance of sweet and bright. The breakfast tacos are loaded with flavor, and if you love variety, their huge build your own omelet selection is a dream. Great food, great energy, great spot.

Inkwell Cafe

$$

★★★★

Inkwell is a popular study spot on campus. Their coffee menu is super fun to explore, and the food is just as good. I love the avocado toast for something fresh or the bacon cheddar scramble when I'm craving a high-protein breakfast.
