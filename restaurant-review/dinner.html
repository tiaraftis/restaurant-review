Da Vinci

Da Vinci is the top dinner pick for many people in Bloomington, and once you try it, you’ll understand why. The Italian inspired menu is loaded with incredible pastas and wood-fired pizzas. My top choices are the Truffle Tortellini and the Pesto Tortellini. For pizza, the Da Vinci is unbeatable and perfect for sharing it has a hot honey drizzle that makes the flavors so complex.

Malibu Grill

Malibu Grill has a diverse menu filled with comforting classics. The focaccia is light and flavorful, and the Spinach & Artichoke Dip is always a win. Their wood-fired pizzas are great, but I’m obsessed with the Garlic Cream Linguine—rich, smooth, and so satisfying.

Siam House

A family-owned gem right off Kirkwood! I always start with the Crab Rangoon or Thai Dumplings. Their Pad Kee Mao Drunken Noodles are my go-to every single time—they’re packed with bold flavor and always cooked perfectly. A cozy, flavorful, reliable favorite.

Janko's Little Zagreb

If you’re looking for a special occasion dinner, Janko’s is worth every penny. The menu is small but expertly done. Start with the Jumbo Shrimp Cocktail, it’s fresh and delicious. The filet truly melts in your mouth and pairs perfectly with their homestyle baked potato. Classic, elevated, and unforgettable.

Ami

Ami is the best spot for sushi in Bloomington, hands down. Whether I’m craving a spicy California roll or some comforting teriyaki chicken, they always deliver. The gyoza is my must order appetizer, crispy, flavorful, and the perfect way to kick off the meal.
