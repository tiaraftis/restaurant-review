LUNCH

Bru Burger $$

★★★★★

Bru Burger is the spot when you want a seriously good burger. Their nachos are the best starter on the menu, loaded, crunchy, and perfect for sharing. My go-to is the Garlic Three Cheese Burger, but the White Cheddar Bacon Burger is right behind it. You truly can’t go wrong here.

Farm $$$

★★★★

Farm offers creative takes on comfort food, and the menu is always exciting. I love starting with the famous Garlic Fries, they pack so much flavor. The Birria Tacos are unmatched in Bloomington, and the Hoosier Legacy Burger comes topped with an incredible bacon jam that makes every bite amazing.

Nicks English Hut $$

★★★★☆

Nick’s truly has a little bit of everything. I always kick things off with their mozzarella sticks, they’re massive and give a perfect cheese pull every time. The pizzas are ideal for sharing, and the fries come in a huge bucket that’s perfect to take on a stroll around Kirkwood. A Bloomington classic for a reason.

Upland Brewery $$

★★★★

Upland’s menu is broad and full of standout dishes. The Whipped Feta appetizer is unforgettable and a must-order. The Wit Mac & Cheese is a classy spin on the comfort dish we all love. If I’m extra hungry, I go for the Blackened Chicken, it’s seasoned perfectly and super flavorful.

BuffaLouies $$

★★★★☆

These are the most iconic wings in Bloomington, and they absolutely earn the hype. They have tons of flavor options, and every single one hits. The Mendoza Burger supports the MS Society, which makes it even better. And when it’s late and I’m craving something salty, the loaded fries or tots are unbeatable.
